What is Frequency?

Simply put, frequency is vibration.

Sound is one form of frequency, but it goes deeper than that. Solid objects, wireless signals, even light and energy all have frequencies. Understanding frequency, cymatics, and sound could help us better understand the universe.

What is Cymatics?

Cymatics is the study of how sound vibrations create visible patterns, such as the images on this page.

Example: sprinkle sand on a metal plate, play a tone, and the sand arranges into geometric shapes.

Different frequencies create different patterns—some simple, some very complex.

Cymatics gives us a hands-on way to see what both physics and metaphysics suggest:

Everything is frequency, and vibration shapes matter and energy into form.
