What is Cymatics?

Cymatics is the study of how sound vibrations create visible patterns, such as the images on this page.

Example: sprinkle sand on a metal plate, play a tone, and the sand arranges into geometric shapes.

Different frequencies create different patterns—some simple, some very complex.
